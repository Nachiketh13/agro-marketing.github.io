About Coconut

The coconut tree is a palm tree.
The coconut tree is long and has leaves at the top.
It has no stem or branches.
The products of a coconut tree have many uses.
The coconut tree is found in more than 90 countries, mainly in the Asia and Pacific regions. Botanically speaking, a coconut is a fibrous one-seeded drupe, also known as a dry drupe. However, when using loose definitions, the coconut can be all three: a fruit, a nut, and a seed.

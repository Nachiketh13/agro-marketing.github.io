Which Season Is Better to Grow RICE

Rice is largely grown under rain fed condition during June-August to October - December. This region comprises of Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala and Tamil Nadu.
Kharif or winter is the main rice growing season in the country. It is known as Winter Rice or Kharif Rice as per the harvesting time. The sowing time of winter (kharif) rice is June-July and it is harvested in November-December.

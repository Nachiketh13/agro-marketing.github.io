Which Season Is Better to Grow SUGARCANE

The normal planting season is October-December. Delay in planting reduces cane yield. Planting should not be delayed beyond February in the plains. In hilly tracts where sugarcane is cultivated under rain fed conditions, planting should be done after abatement of heavy rains.Sugarcane take generally one year to mature in subtropical states of U.P., Punjab, Haryana, Bihar etc. Depending upon the variety and sowing time it takes about 12 to 18 months to mature. In general January to March is the period of planting and December to March is the period of harvesting.
